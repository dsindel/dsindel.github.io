Debug: show ▼
  • Latitude macros:
    • D: {latdegabs}: '0', {latdegdec}: '0', {latdegint}: '0', {latdeground}: '0', {latdegdecabs}: '0'
    • M: {latmindec}: '0', {latminint}: '0', {latminround}: '0'
    • M:2: {latmindec:2}: '00', {latminint:2}: '00', {latminround:2}: '00'
    • S: {latsecdec}: '0', {latsecint}: '0', {latsecround}: '0'
    • S:2: {latsecdec:2}: '00', {latsecint:2}: '00', {latsecround:2}: '00'
    • N/S: {latNS}: 'N', {% (latNS == 'N' ? 's.š.' : 'j.š.') %}: 's.š.'
  • Longitude macros:
    • D: {londegabs}: '0', {londegdec}: '0', {londegint}: '0', {londeground}: '0', {londegdecabs}: '0'
    • M: {lonmindec}: '0', {lonminint}: '0', {lonminround}: '0'
    • M:2: {lonmindec:2}: '00', {lonminint:2}: '00', {lonminround:2}: '00'
    • S: {lonsecdec}: '0', {lonsecint}: '0', {lonsecround}: '0'
    • S:2: {lonsecdec:2}: '00', {lonsecint:2}: '00', {lonsecround:2}: '00'
    • E/W: {lonEW}: 'E', {% (lonEW == 'E' ? 'v.d.' : 'z.d.') %}: 'v.d.'
  • Other macros:
    • {type}: ''
    • {dim}: '', {dimm}: '', {dimmint}: ''
    • {scale}: '10000', {span}: '0.01', {osmzoom}: '15'
    • {region}: ''
    • {globe}: ''
    • {heading}: ''
    • {elevation}: '{elevation}', {elevationint}: '{elevationint}'
    • {ghparams}: '', {ghparamsp}: '', {ghparamsu}: '' (incl. heading)
    • {ghparamsnh}: '', {ghparamsnhp}: '', {ghparamsnhu}: '' (excl. heading)
    • {ghparamsnt}: '', {ghparamsntp}: '', {ghparamsntu}: '' (excl. type)
    • {selfurl}: 'http://tools.freeside.sk/geolocator/geolocator.html?params=0.000000_0.000000&dp=6&sp=2&mp=2&hp=16&rm='
    • {selfurlparams}: 'params=0.000000_0.000000&dp=6&sp=2&mp=2&hp=16&rm='
